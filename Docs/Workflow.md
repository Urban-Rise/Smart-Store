# Workflow

Smart-Store operational flow:

1. Browse catalog and categories.
2. View product details, specifications, and images.
3. Add items to cart.
4. Proceed to checkout and payment.
5. Track orders, invoices, and installments.
6. Engage with reviews and community feedback.
7. Access empowerment messages and symbolic guidance.
8. Explore 3D Printing Hub for design upload and simulation.
